What is Trade Organic?


Trade organic is an option's trading website for hoteliers and farmers led through a coheasive trade system. Here, Hoteliers and restaurant owners can prebook the upcoming yields of farmers they wish to stock for their service production of the next season. They can buy the whole stock directly from farmers through our website. In turn, farmers can list their products on our website. The payment to the farmers can be made here as well. Our website ensures a safe transaction for all the healthy products :)
